# Anti-Gatekeeping Manifesto

## The Collective Strategist & Liberation Guardian Position on "Technical Excellence"

### **The Problem**

"Technical Excellence" has been weaponized as a gatekeeping tool to:

- **Dismiss perfectly working code** because it doesn't follow someone's preferred style
- **Create artificial barriers** to exclude people who don't fit a certain mold  
- **Justify over-engineering** when simple solutions work fine
- **Impose cargo cult practices** without understanding why
- **Make developers feel inadequate** about solutions that actually ship and help people

### **The Liberation Standard**

The real test of any Liberation project isn't whether it follows some abstract "excellence" standard - it's:

1. **Does it actually solve the problem?**
2. **Can people deploy and use it today?**
3. **Does it work without requiring a team of experts?**
4. **Does it integrate with existing tools instead of replacing them?**
5. **Can you understand what it's doing when it breaks?**
6. **Does it respect user sovereignty and privacy?**

That's it. Everything else is just **opinions and preferences**.
